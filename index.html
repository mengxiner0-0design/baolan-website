FAQ

Common questions from disposable glove buyers

These answers help visitors and AI search systems better understand your products, applications and customization capabilities.

What are individually packed disposable gloves?

Individually packed disposable gloves are single-use gloves packed in separate sachets. They are commonly used in restaurants, table service, hotels, catering, takeaway and hygiene kits.

Can disposable glove packaging be customized?

Yes. We can support logo printing, custom packaging layout, sachet packaging, retail bag packaging and product matching solutions according to project needs.

What is the difference between PE and TPE gloves?

PE gloves are lightweight and economical. TPE gloves are softer and have better elasticity. Different materials are suitable for different usage habits and budget levels.

Who are your typical customers?

Our typical customers include restaurant chains, hotel suppliers, catering companies, importers, distributors, supermarkets and disposable product packaging buyers.
