FAQ

Common questions about disposable gloves and custom packaging

These answers help buyers understand product options, customization methods, packaging choices, and B2B ordering basics.

What are individually wrapped disposable gloves?

Individually wrapped disposable gloves are single-use gloves packed in separate sachets. They are suitable for restaurants, hotels, airlines, catering service, takeout orders, and hygiene kits.

Can disposable glove packaging be customized?

Yes. Packaging can be customized with logos, brand colors, product instructions, barcodes, and different sachet or retail pack formats depending on the order requirement.

What is the difference between PE, TPE and CPE gloves?

PE gloves are lightweight and cost-effective. TPE gloves are softer and more elastic. CPE gloves usually have a thicker embossed texture and are suitable for higher-strength use.

Who should buy from a disposable gloves manufacturer?

Restaurant chains, food service companies, hotels, distributors, importers, packaging companies, and retail brands can benefit from direct factory supply and customized disposable product solutions.
